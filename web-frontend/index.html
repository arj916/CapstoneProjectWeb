Canopy Power: A Solar-Powered Beach Umbrella

How To

  1. Connect to the Umbrella:
    • Click the Connect to Umbrella button.
    • Select the device from the list of available Bluetooth devices.
    • Once connected, the BLE state will change to Connected.
  2. Check Battery Level:
    • After connecting, the battery level will automatically update under Battery Level.
  3. Earn Points:
    • Use the umbrella to charge your devices and earn points for every watt-hour of energy used.
    • Your total points will display under Points Earned.
  4. Track Fossil Fuel Savings:
    • Your usage will also calculate fossil fuel savings, displayed under Fossil Fuel Savings.
  5. Disconnect from the Umbrella:
    • Click the Disconnect from Umbrella button to end the session.
    • The BLE state will change back to Disconnected.

Did You Know?

Using solar energy to charge devices helps reduce reliance on fossil fuels and decreases greenhouse gas emissions.

On average, 386 grams of CO2 are emitted per kWh in the US, and charging one smartphone consumes about 2-4 kWh per year!
